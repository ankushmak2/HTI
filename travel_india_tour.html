How does it work

Book With Confident

Book with Confidence We want you to feel confident that when you book your India Tour with us, you're in safe hands. We are proud to be a member of all the key trade associations and regulatory bodies within the travel industry, ensuring you a high standard of service to our valuable guests.

We are a member of:

  • Indian Association of Tour Operators - IATO
  • Association of Domestic Tour Operators of India - ADTOI
  • Travel Agents Federation of India - TAFI

BOOKING PROCEDURE



STEP 1: OFFICIAL QUOTATION
If you are interested in one of our services or would like us to send you a custom proposal based on your requirements, please complete the request form provided on the contact us page. We will then send you an official tour quotation based on your request shortly after receiving your enquiry.
STEP 2: ACCEPTANCE OF OUR QUOTATION
In our correspondence with you we may suggest numerous proposals until you are happy with the details provided in a specific quotation. You may then send us an e-mail confirming that you are happy with the quotation and would like to book the services offered.
STEP 3: RESERVATION CONFIRMATION & DEPOSIT
India tour explorer will send you a Reservation Confirmation email. after we have booked all of the necessary accommodations and other services.
Along with the Reservation Confirmation, we will forward OUR INVOICE with a bank details or a credit card authorization form that can be used to make the 60% deposit payment. The credit card authorization form can be proper fill out & send us a scanned copy with Signature and emailed to us. Alternatively, you also have the option of completing a wired money transfer directly to our bank account with the help of SWIFT CODE . Our banking details will be included in the INVOICE.
STEP 4: FINAL PAYMENT
The final payment for your India Tour is required 30 days prior to the starting date of the tour.
STEP 5: ARRIVAL IN INDIA
Upon arrival in India, you will be met to our representative at the airport, holding a sign with your name on it. If for any reason you do not immediately see your representative, a cell phone number is provided on the Reservation Confirmation which can be used to contact our Operations Manager. The representative will provide you with a file containing the Reservation Confirmation you received via e-mail, as well as all necessary vouchers.
From here onward you are in the care of an experienced and in safe hands. Either you have booked a escorted India tours or Private India Tours, the representative will review the itinerary and route with you and also handover all the necessary documents.
