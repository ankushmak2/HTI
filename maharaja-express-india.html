Palace on Wheels

Embark on a royal journey on the most renowned Palace on Wheels luxury train of India. Travel through the land of erstwhile Kings of Rajasthan, visit some of the most spectacular forts, palaces, temples and wildlife of the this colourful state, experience great food, great hospitality and ethnic interior decor onboard. A visit to Taj Mahal gives a perfect finishing touch to your journey on Palace on Wheels luxury train.

Royal Rajasthan on Wheels

This newly built luxury train of India is an extensively planned Indian luxury train tour through the beautiful deserts, royal palaces and massive forts of Rajasthan, amazingly carved temples of Khajuraho and the holiest city of Varanasi. This is a seven days exploration which may be a short lived journey but will be remembered for long by those who experience it.

Read More

Maharaja's Express

India's answer to the Orient Express of the west, the Maharaja's Express Indian luxury train offers five different pan-Indian itineraries to suit with your interest. Covering some of the most prominent Indian destinations across country, the Maharaja's Express luxury train offers you different itineraries for 04 days and 08 days so that you can enjoy an Indian luxury train tour depending on your schedule and time.

More Route

Splendor of The South

The Golden Chariot luxury train of South India is named after the world heritage site and world famous stone chariot of Hampi in Karnataka. Tastefully designed on Hoysala temple architecture this beautiful train offers 2 tastefully designed luxury train tours in South India. This is undoubtedly a moment filled worth cherishing journey in Indian luxury train.

Read More

Pride of The South

The trip Pride of South on Golden Chariot luxury train is an exploration through some finest historic and spiritual sites and wildlife reserve of Karnataka and gorgeous beaches of Goa. The week long journey trip flags off from Bangalore and travels through Bandipur Wildlife Park, Srirangapatanam, Mysore, Sravanbelagola, Belur, Halebidu, Badami, Pattdakal and Goa.

Read More

Deccan Odyssey

Experience the historical, spiritual, cultural and wild side of India as you travel through the legendry state of Maharashtra. The Deccan Odyssey luxury train of India offers 10 different tour itineraries including a short 02 night trip to Shirdi temple and 03 nights Golden Triangle Tour of India with tiger watching.
