South India Tour

Welcome to Incredible INDIA – the place where heaven falls here and a place where you can console your soul, body and mind. Vacations in India: means the world’s most ancient culture and mediaeval architecture; divine rivers and herd, streets; religious rituals, festivals, urban / rural life and much more. Undoubtedly India is prodigious landmass termed a subcontinent is known for its Colourful people, nature, culture, fair and festivals, Forts, Palaces, architecture, Temples, beaches, music, dances, art and humanity to make India the most tremendous country. Indian culture is considered to be amongst the world’s oldest, richest and most diverse culture.

Experience one of the Best INDIAN GOLDEN TRIANGLE TOUR with the and ready to explore the real India : This tour starts and ends in Delhi, India.

This journey is through the most popular tour circuit of India known as the Golden Triangle , which is the combinen of the DELHI , AGRA , JAIPUR to make it different we will visit few more interesting places called ABHANERI a stepwells, Fatehpur Sikri , Choki Dhani , Monkey Temple.

Delhi is a bustling metropolis and the capital region of India, which includes the national capital city, New Delhi. The area around Delhi was probably inhabited before the second millennium BC, and there is evidence of continuous habitation since at least the 6th century BC. The city is believed to be the site of Indraprastha, the legendary capital of the Pandavas in the Indian epic Mahabharata. Through most of its history, Delhi has served as a capital of various kingdoms and empires. It has been captured, sacked and rebuilt several times, particularly during the medieval period, and therefore the modern conurbation of Delhi is a cluster of a number of cities spread across the metropolitan region.

The splendour of Agra – capital of all India under the Mughals – remains undiminished, from the massive fort to the magnificent Taj Mahal. The Taj Mahal is regarded as one of the wonders of the world, and historians have noted that its architectural beauty has never been surpassed. The Taj Mahal was built by a Muslim, Emperor Shah Jahan in the memory of his dear wife and queen Mumtaz Mahal. It is a Mausoleum that houses the grave of queen Mumtaz Mahal at the lower chamber. The Taj Mahal was constructed over a period of twenty-two years, employing twenty thousand workers. It was completed in A.D. 1648.

The Taj Mahal is built entirely of white marble. Its stunning architectural beauty is beyond adequate description, particularly at dawn and sunset. The Taj seems to glow in the light of the full moon. On a foggy morning, the visitors experience the Taj as if suspended when viewed from across the Jamuna river.

Jaipur, also known as ‘Pink City’ was founded by Maharaja Jai Singh II in A. D. 1727. Jaipur is famous for its rich cultural heritage and has today become the most popular tourist destination in India. Here one gets to see the architectural marvels of ancient forts and monuments that stand tall to this day. The city has acquired the name Pink City because of the extensive use of pink color on the walls during the visit of the Prince of Wales. The city is garlanded by the presence of a number of beautiful monuments that echo the splendid history of the city. It is no surprise then that tourists coming to this city always feel the urge to come back.

Detailed Tour Itinerary

DAY 01 : Arrives Chennai
on arrival at Chennai and you will be welcome by our representative and introduce your driver and check in to the hotel and after refreshment proceed to visit famous Kapaleeswar Temple dedicated to is Lord Kapaleeswar (Lord Siva). Gopuram, Mandapam and tank are interesting points to see, then visit Vadapalani Murugan temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, you can spend sometimes at the famous Elliots Beach in Chennai, later in the afternoon you have day free for shopping you can visit T. Nagar it is a best and one single place where we can buy various commodities, after shopping return back to the hotel and stay overnight in hotel
DAY 02: Chennai/Mahabalipuram
Morning after breakfast start visiting the famous coastal part in Tamil nadu which is also the land depict the history of Pallava rulers, up on arrival check in to the hotel and after small refreshment proceed to visit the famous sea shore Temples, It is now listed as one of the world heritage sites of the UNESCO. Numerous carvings can be found throughout the temple later stay overnight in hotel
DAY 03:Mahabalipuram/Pondicherry
Morning after breakfast start drive to Pondicherry which is popular said to be as French territory in South India , up on arrival check in to the hotel and in the evening you can walk around the French quarters, stay overnight in hotel
DAY 04: Pondicherry
you can visit Mankula vinayagar temple and Arabindo Ashram and stay overnight at Hotel
DAY 05:Pondicherry/Tanjore
after breakfast start drive to Tanjore is one of the oldest cities in India and has a long and varied history dating back to Sangam period, The city rose into prominence during the rule of the Later Cholas when it served as the capital of the Chola Empire, visit Brigadeeshwara temple,This 10th century temple is a part of the UNESCO world heritage site ‘’Great living Chola Temple’’ the name Periya Kovil came from its original name ‘’Periya aavudayar Kovil’’. The vimana (Main Tower) of the temple is approximately 65meters (215ft) high and it is the tallest in the world. It was so designed that the vimana never casts a shadow at noon during any period of the year and stay overnight at Tanjore
DAY 06:Tanjore/Trichy
start drive to Trichy City tour visit Srirangam Temple, surrounded by the waters of the River Kaveri on one side & its tributary Kollidam on the other, is a 600-acre island-town enclosed within the seven walls of the gigantic Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple. There are not less than 21 Gopuram here in this temple, one of which is the tallest in South India. Later we visit Rock fort most famed landmark is an 83 m high rock which is the only outcrop in the otherwise flat land of the city. The most amazing fact about the rock is that it is one of the oldest in the world approximately 3,800 million years, which makes it as old as the rocks of Greenland & older than the Himalayas, in the evening you can spend your time for shopping in Trichy, stay overnight in hotel.
DAY 07:Trichy/Madurai
Today morning drive to visit famous Madurai Meenakshiamman Temple in the day time which is one of the famous temple in South India, later visit Thirumalanayak palace - This palace was built by King Thirumalai Nayak, who was one of the Nayak ruler, later visit Gandhi Museum - This museum was built to honor our Father of nation, Gandhi JI and in the in the evening visit Night Aarti ceremony held at Meenakshiamman temple, stay overnight in Madurai
DAY 08:Madurai/Ooty
Today Morning after breakfast start drive to Ooty better known as the queen of hill stations. At a height of 2,240m, Ooty is loved for its salubrious climate & scenic beauty on arrival check in to the hotel and rest of the day at leisure stay overnight in Hotel
DAY 9: Ooty
Today Morning after breakfast drive to Ooty Train station to enjoy the Toy train ride from Ooty to Coonoor, toy train ride in Ooty is an another major attraction in Ooty, this train path in mountain region was formed during British period and now it becomes a major attraction for tourists, while travelling in the train you can view the beautiful sceneries presented in the hill station, it must be a pleasant experience for those whoever take this toy train ride, up on arrival you can visit proceed for Coonoor sightseeing visit Lambs rock, dolphin nose and then return back to Ooty visit famous government botanical garden This beautiful garden has spread over 55 acres, the garden is a home to several rare species of trees such as the cork tree ( the only one of its kind in India), the paper bark tree and the monkey puzzle tree, so named because monkeys cannot climb it! Also to be found are the remnants of a fossilized tree estimated to be some 20 million years old! Then you can visit the famous Rose garden which was set up in 1955 to commemorate the 100th flower show of the botanical garden, it has the largest collection of roses in the country of over 2500 Varieties, later you can drive to Ooty lake This is artificial lake which was created by John Sullivan, the first district collector of Udhagamandalam in 1824, the boating will starts from 0900AM to 0600PM on all days in the lake. A wide range of boats are available at the boat house to suit varied tastes and budgets. A mini garden with an amusement park for children and a toy train are added attractions here. Stay overnight in Ooty
DAY 10:Ooty/Coimbatore/Cochin – Train
start drive to Coimbatore and you will board overnight train to Cochin and night will be at Train
DAY 11:Cochin/Munnar
Today morning up on arrival drive to Munnar the most famous hill station in Kerala up on arrival check in to the hotel and rest of the day at leisure you can spend your time by walking besides the lush green tea plantations which catches the eye appeal for anyone who visit Munnar, stay overnight at Hotel.
DAY 12:Munnar
Morning post your breakfast you can enjoy the sightseeing of Munnar visit famous Eravikulam National park where you can see a rare species Mountain goats (Nilgiri tahr), later drive to Mattupatty Dam which is consider as the beautiful dam in Munnar where you have the option to enjoy the boating on the dam water, also you can visit the rose garden located near to this dam, then you can visit the Tea museum where you can learn the history of Tea and its cultivation and processing, later return back to the hotel and stay overnight in Munnar
DAY 13:Munnar/Thekkady
after breakfast start drive to Periyar, the most famous wild life region in Kerala up on arrival check in to the hotel, in the evening you can go for spice plantation tour where you can able to see the variety of spices grown in Periyar and its major uses in Cooking stay overnight Hotel
DAY 14:Thekkady
early morning @ 0700AM you can enjoy the boating provided inside the wild life sanctuary, where by sitting inside the boat you have the option to enjoy the wild animals freely roams inside the forest, later return back to the hotel and post your breakfast later you can enjoy the program provided at the Elephant camp at Periyar, you can experience the activities such as Elephant bathing, feeding, timber dragging, photo session with tea & coffee, in the evening you have the option to witness the kalaripayattu Marital art show after dinner stay overnight in Periyar
DAY 15:Thekkady/Alleppey
Morning post your breakfast proceed to Alleppey the famous backwater region in Kerala, up on arrival check in to the house boat and enjoy the beauty of natural backwaters from the house boat, hot meals will be served to you in the house boat, as a newly married couple you can enjoy the candle light dinner exclusively arranged for you, stay overnight in the house boat
DAY 16:Alleppey/Cherai
after disembark drive Cherai one of the famous beach destination and up on arrival stay over night
DAY 17:Cherai
Day is free for leisure and stay over night
DAY 18:Cherai /Cochin – Departure
Morning proceed to Cochin you can spend your time for shopping and in the evening after dinner you will transferred on time to Airport to board your flight with sweet memories of your holiday.
